How Vexis Works

Raw sensor data → Actionable health insights

01

Connect OBD Scanner

Plug any USB ELM327 scanner into your vehicle's OBD-II port and connect via USB to start streaming sensor data.

02

AI Analyzes Data

Isolation Forest ML models process 21 derived features across 5 vehicle systems to detect anomalies and health patterns.

03

Get Health Score

Receive component scores 0-100, detected issues, failure risk predictions and download a detailed PDF health report.
